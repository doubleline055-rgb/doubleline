FAQ

Questions, answered

Is this really a kosher line — no internet at all?

Correct. Dubole Line runs on kosher-certified devices with no data access, so there's no internet, browser, or app store — calling and texting only.

How do people in the US call me without paying extra?

Your line comes with a US-reachable number alongside your Israeli number. When someone in the US calls that number, it's treated like a normal domestic US call on their end.

Shipping times depend on your location — this will be confirmed at checkout.
